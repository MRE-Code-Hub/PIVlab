Journal papers etc. that used PIVlab

I am really happy to see that there is a huge amount of studies (> 3000) that use PIVlab for all kinds of different experiments (fluid dynamics, sand and gravel motion, cell motion, blood flow, flow mapping of rivers, 3D printing, dam breaching etc.).
